Why IOT is requiring us to rething resiliency at the edge

This session will address availability levels needed to support new data demands such as smart cities and remote business branch offices.
Learn how business critical edge sites should be designed to achieve high availability as the current paradigm of data center availability is inadequate; its metric is not comprehensive as it does not consider user-interruption based on business criticality and the number of people per site affected.
